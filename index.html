About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with over 8 years of experience creating digital experiences that bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality. My work is influenced by my Spanish heritage, the vibrant streets of Madrid, and a deep appreciation for minimalist design principles.

My approach combines strategic thinking with artistic expression, ensuring that every project not only looks beautiful but also serves its intended purpose effectively. I believe that good design should be intuitive, accessible, and emotionally resonant.

This website represents my ongoing exploration of form, color, and interaction. Each project showcased here tells a story of collaboration, experimentation, and the pursuit of visual harmony in an increasingly digital world.

My Design Philosophy

Behind every portfolio is a point of view. A great project goes beyond aesthetics to offer a glimpse of the person behind the work. Is it a particular artistic movement? A way of experiencing the world? What drives these things to be the way they are? My work seeks to answer these questions through intentional design that balances form, function, and feeling.
